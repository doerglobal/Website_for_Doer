What is doer ideation challenge?

The Doer Ideation Challenge is a comprehensive process of idea identification and the recognition of potential entrepreneurs within college campuses. It aims to educate, empower, and support students in problem identification, gaining insights about the problem, and developing unique solutions.


Benefits of participation in Antah Prerana | अंतः प्रेरणा

Doer Smart Incubation Program

Get complete access to the Doer Smart Incubation Program, our flagship entrepreneurship development program, designed to accelerate fundraising for early-stage founders from colleges/universities across Northeast India.

Instant Rewards and Recognition

Doer will reward the top 3 (three) ideas with exciting prizes and also recognize everyone with an awesome certificate as a tangible token of their entrepreneurial and problem solving spirit.



Enhance your problem-solving & Presentation skills

Doer will take you on an incredible journey of rapid problem-solving, empowering every student to unlock their potential with critical thinking and problem-solving skills, paving the way for a successful future together!

Receive Feedback and Guidances

At Doer, we cherish your ideas, nurturing and protecting them as they progress. Within 30 days of the "Doer Ideation Challenge," you'll receive valuable feedback and suggestions. Your journey to innovation and entrepreneurship is our top priority!

Online registration opens: August 19, 2023

Participants can register online through the Doer Ideation Challenge website. The registration form will ask for basic information such as name, email address, and contact details.

Ideation challenge: September 9th, 2023

The ideation challenge will be at NERIST campus and participants will have the opportunity to present their ideas to a panel of judges. The judges will be looking for ideas that are creative, innovative, and have the potential to make a difference in rural livelihoods.

Shortlist Announcement: September 9th, 2023

The shortlisted participants/teams will be notified by email. The shortlisted participants/teams will have the opportunity to get full access to the Doer Smart Incubation Program.

Doer Smart Incubation starts: Date to be announced

The Doer Smart Incubation Program is a 6-month program that provides participants with the resources and mentorship they need to turn their ideas into investment ready. The program includes workshops, training, and access to a network of industry experts.
